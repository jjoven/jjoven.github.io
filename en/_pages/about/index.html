SUMMARY

For the past 8+ years I've worked with non-profits, small businesses, and start-ups, focusing on scaling organizations through business process optimization and CRM design & implementation. I love using my analytical skills to improve the flow of data between different parts of an organization and help teams work more cohesively.


I've worked as a Salesforce consultant and as the primary architect and administrator for a 150+ user Salesforce environment, including Visualforce and Apex development. I'm also a certified Dell Boomi Professional Developer with experience building custom integrations between CRM, accounting and construction management platforms.


I care about living my life with minimal negative impact to the environment and prioritize working with mission based organizations committed to sustainability.
